      In Car Escape, your goal is to navigate your car through a series of obstacles to reach the finish line.

      1. Start by selecting your car and level.
      2. Use the arrow keys to control your car:
      - Up Arrow to accelerate
      - Down Arrow to brake/reverse
      - Left Arrow to turn left
      - Right Arrow to turn right
