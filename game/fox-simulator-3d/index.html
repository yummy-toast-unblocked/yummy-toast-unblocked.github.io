      Instructions

      Objective:
      Outwit other players by deducing their actions and strategically choosing your own to gain points and win the game.

      Setup:
      1. Each player receives a set of action cards.
      2. Shuffle the deck of event cards and place it in the center.
      3. Each player starts with 10 points.

      Gameplay:
      1. At the beginning of each round, draw an event card and read it aloud. This card will affect the round's actions.
      2. Players secretly choose one action card from their hand and place it face down.
      3. Once all players have chosen, everyone reveals their action cards simultaneously.
      4. Resolve the actions based on the event card and the actions chosen by the players.
      5. Adjust points according to the outcomes specified by the event card and player interactions.
      6. Discard the used event card and proceed to the next round.

      Winning the Game:
      The game continues for a predetermined number of rounds or until a player reaches a certain number of points. The player with the most points at the end wins.

      FAQs:

      Q: What happens if there's a tie at the end of the game?
      A: If there's a tie, the player who accumulated the most points in the final round wins. If still tied, consider the game a shared victory.

      Q: Can I play the same action card multiple times?
      A: Yes, you can play the same action card as many times as you wish, but strategic diversity may benefit you.

      Q: What if an event card's instructions are unclear?
      A: If the event card's instructions are unclear, players should collectively agree on an interpretation or consult the rulebook for clarification.

      Q: How many players can play?
      A: The game is designed for 2 to 6 players.

      Q: What should I do if I lose an action card?
      A: If an action card is lost, substitute it with a proxy card or make a temporary replacement until it can be found or replaced.
