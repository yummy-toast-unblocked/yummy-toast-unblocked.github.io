      1. Controls:
      - Move: Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate your character.
      - Jump: Press the spacebar to jump over obstacles.
      - Attack: Click the left mouse button to attack enemies.
      - Special Ability: Press 'E' to activate your character's special ability.

      2. Gameplay:
      - Begin each mission by following the on-screen prompts.
      - Navigate through the environment, avoiding hazards such as spikes, pits, and enemy traps.
