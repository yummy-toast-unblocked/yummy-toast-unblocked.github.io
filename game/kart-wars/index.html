      Fireboy and Watergirl

      Description

      5.0 (3728 Reviews)

      Fireboy and Watergirl is a thrilling puzzle-platformer where teamwork is essential. Navigate through intricate temples filled with traps and obstacles, using Fireboy's resistance to fire and Watergirl's immunity to water. Solve challenging puzzles by leveraging their unique abilities, and collect diamonds to unlock new levels. Will you master the elements and guide them both to safety?


      Instructions

      Fireboy and Watergirl is a cooperative platform game where you control two characters to navigate through puzzles and obstacles. Fireboy can walk through lava but must avoid water, while Watergirl can touch water but must avoid lava.

      1. Move Fireboy using the arrow keys:
      - Left/Right Arrows to move left/right.
      - Up Arrow to jump.

      2. Move Watergirl using the WASD keys:
      - A/D to move left/right.
      - W to jump.

      3. Collect the corresponding gems: red gems for Fireboy and blue gems for Watergirl.

      4. Cooperate to solve puzzles, such as pushing buttons, pulling levers, and moving platforms.

      5. Avoid hazards: Fireboy must avoid water, and Watergirl must avoid lava. Both must avoid green goo.

      6. Reach the exit doors: Guide both characters to their respective exit doors to complete the level.

      7. Work together to complete each level by using both characters' abilities to overcome obstacles.

      Remember, teamwork is essential!
