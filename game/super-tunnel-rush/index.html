      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Setup: Divide into two teams and choose a "car" (a player) for each team.
      * Gameplay: The car must "drive" to designated checkpoints by crawling or walking.
      * Objective: Be the first team to have their car complete all checkpoints.
      * Rules:
      * Cars can only move forward.
      * Cars cannot cut corners.
      * Teams can help their car by pushing or pulling from behind.
      * If a car falls over, it must start from the beginning of the current checkpoint.
      FAQs:
      * Q: Can multiple cars race at the same time?
      * A: Yes, but it is recommended to have only one car racing per team at a time.
      * Q: What happens if two cars reach a checkpoint at the same time?
      * A: Both teams get credit for completing the checkpoint.
      * Q: Can teams change their car during the game?
      * A: Yes, but only between checkpoints.
