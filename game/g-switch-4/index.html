      Instructions

      Gather your friends and prepare for a fun and engaging game. Follow these instructions to play:

      1. Setup:
      - Each player selects a character card.
      - Place the game board in the center and distribute the starting resources as per the game's rulebook.
      - Shuffle and place the event and action decks on the designated spots.

      2. Objective:
      - The goal is to accumulate the most points by the end of the game. Points can be earned through completing quests, acquiring resources, and achieving character-specific goals.

      3. Turn Sequence:
      - On a player's turn, they can perform up to three actions, which include:
      - Move: Move your character to an adjacent location on the board.
      - Collect Resources: Gather resources available in your current location.
      - Complete Quests: Meet the requirements of a quest card and claim it for points.
      - Use Abilities: Use your character's unique abilities as described on their card.
      - After taking actions, draw an event card and resolve its effects.

      4. End of Game:
      - The game ends after a set number of rounds or when a specific event card is drawn.
      - Calculate each player's points based on quests completed, resources gathered, and character goals achieved.
      - The player with the highest score wins.

      FAQs:

      1. How many players can play?
      - The game is designed for 2 to 6 players.

      2. How long does a game typically last?
      - A game usually lasts between 45 to 90 minutes, depending on the number of players and their familiarity with the rules.

      3. What happens if there is a tie in points at the end of the game?
      - In the event of a tie, the player with the most completed quests wins. If still tied, the player with the most resources wins.

      4. Can I use more than one character ability in a single turn?
      - No, you can only use one character ability per turn unless a card or event specifies otherwise.

      5. What should I do if I can't complete any quests during my turn?
      - You can focus on gathering resources or moving to a new location to set up for future turns.

      6. Are there penalties for not drawing an event card at the end of my turn?
      - Yes, forgetting to draw an event card results in losing one action in your next turn.

      7. Can resources be traded between players?
      - No, resources cannot be traded between players.

      8. What if I run out of resource tokens?
      - If resource tokens run out, use a substitute or track resources on paper until tokens become available again.

      9. Can I skip my turn?
      - No, skipping a turn is not allowed. However, you can choose to take fewer actions if you wish.

      10. Are there expansions available for this game?
      - Yes, there are several expansions that add new characters, quests, and mechanics to the game.
