      Bubble Shooter

      Description

      5.0 (3643 Reviews)

      Immerse yourself in the addictive world of Bubble Shooter, where quick thinking and precise aiming are your best allies! In this vibrant and colorful game, your mission is to match and pop bubbles by shooting them into clusters of the same color. With each successful match, watch as the bubbles burst in a satisfying cascade, earning you points and clearing the board. Strategize your shots to create massive chain reactions and unlock special power-ups. Bubble Shooter is easy to learn but challenging to master, offering endless fun for players of all ages!


      Instructions

      In Bubble Shooter, your goal is to clear the board by popping bubbles.

      1. Aim your shooter by moving your mouse to position the arrow in the direction you want to shoot.
      2. Click your mouse to launch a bubble from the shooter.
      3. Match three or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop and disappear.
      4. Clear all the bubbles on the board to advance to the next level.
      5. Watch out for the wall at the bottom; if the bubbles reach it, the game is over.

      Use strategy to create chain reactions and clear the board efficiently.
