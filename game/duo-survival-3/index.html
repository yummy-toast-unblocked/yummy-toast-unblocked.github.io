      Instructions

      Instructions:
      1. Divide the deck evenly between two players.
      2. Players keep their cards hidden.
      3. Simultaneously, each player flips their top card face up.
      4. The player with the higher-ranking card takes both cards and adds them to their pile.
      5. If the cards are of equal rank, there is a "war."
      6. Each player places three cards face down, then flips their fourth card face up.
      7. The player with the higher-ranking fourth card wins the war and takes all the cards.
      8. The game continues until one player has all the cards.
      FAQs:

      * What if a player runs out of cards during a war?
      * The player with cards wins the war.
      * Can players see each other's cards?
      * No, cards remain hidden until they are flipped face up.
      * What is the goal of the game?
      * To collect all the cards.
