      Instructions

      Objective: Reach the finish line before your opponents.

      Setup:
      1. Each player selects a car and places it on the starting line.
      2. Shuffle and place the deck of action cards face down.

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      2. On your turn, draw an action card and follow its instructions. Action cards may include:
      - Move Forward: Move your car the indicated number of spaces.
      - Move Backward: Move your car the indicated number of spaces.
      - Boost: Move forward an additional number of spaces specified.
      - Obstacle: Place an obstacle on any space on the track.
      - Repair: Remove an obstacle from the track.
      3. If you land on an obstacle, follow the penalty on the obstacle card.
      4. The first player to reach the finish line wins the game.

      Additional Rules:
      - If there is a tie at the finish line, the player with the highest sum of their last three action cards wins.
      - If you land on the same space as another car, both cars move back to the nearest empty space.

      Enjoy the race and may the best driver win!
