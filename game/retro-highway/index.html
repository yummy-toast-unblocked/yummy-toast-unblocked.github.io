      Instructions

      In the game ''. players take turns drawing cards from a deck and performing the actions indicated on the cards. The objective is to be the last player standing by strategically using the cards to outsmart and outlast your opponents.

      How to Play:
      1. Shuffle the deck and place it in the center of the table.
      2. Each player draws five cards to start.
      3. On your turn, draw one card from the deck.
      4. Play one card from your hand by placing it face up in front of you and following the instructions on the card.
      5. Discard the played card into a discard pile.
      6. Some cards allow you to draw additional cards, skip turns, or force other players to discard cards.
      7. The game continues in turns until only one player remains with cards in their hand.

      FAQs:

      Q: What happens if the draw deck runs out?
      A: Shuffle the discard pile to form a new draw deck and continue playing.

      Q: Can I play more than one card per turn?
      A: No, players are allowed to play only one card per turn unless a card specifically states otherwise.

      Q: What if I can't play any card from my hand?
      A: If you cannot play a card, you must skip your turn and draw two cards as a penalty.

      Q: Can I look at the discard pile?
      A: Players are not allowed to look through the discard pile unless a card permits it.

      Q: How do I win the game?
      A: You win by being the last player with cards in your hand after all other players have been eliminated.
