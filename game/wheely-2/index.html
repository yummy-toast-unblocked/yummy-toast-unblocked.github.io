      Objective:
      Navigate your car through various levels, avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups to reach the finish line as quickly as possible.

      Instructions:
      1. Start the Game: Press the 'Start' button on the main menu.
      2. Choose Your Car: Select your preferred car from the available options.
      3. Begin Level: Once the level loads, your car will appear at the starting line.
      4. Control the Car:
      - Use the arrow keys to steer the car.
      - The 'Up' arrow accelerates.
      - The 'Down' arrow brakes and reverses.
      - The 'Left' and 'Right' arrows steer the car.
      5. Avoid Obstacles: Navigate around obstacles like cones, barriers, and other vehicles.
      6. Collect Power-Ups: Drive through power-ups to gain temporary boosts such as speed, shield, or extra points.
      7. Reach the Finish Line: Drive towards the finish line as fast as you can to complete the level.
      8. Advance Levels: Each completed level unlocks the next, with increasing difficulty.
      9. Pause/Resume: Press the 'P' key to pause or resume the game.
      10. Game Over: If you crash too many times or run out of time, the game will end. Restart the level to try again.
