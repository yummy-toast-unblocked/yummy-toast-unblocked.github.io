      Instructions

      Instructions:

      1. Setup: Shuffle the deck of Car cards and deal 5 cards to each player. Place the remaining deck face down in the center as the draw pile.

      2. Starting the Game: The player to the left of the dealer starts. Play proceeds clockwise.

      3. Objective: Be the first player to complete a full car by collecting all the necessary parts: Engine, Body, Wheels, and Interior.

      4. On Your Turn:
      - Draw one card from the draw pile or the discard pile.
      - Check if you can complete any car parts with your current hand.
      - If you have a complete car part, place it face up in front of you.
      - Discard one card to end your turn.

      5. Completing a Car:
      - To complete a car, you need to have one card each for Engine, Body, Wheels, and Interior.
      - Once you have all four parts, announce "Car Complete!" and place all four cards face up in front of you.

      6. Winning the Game: The first player to complete a car wins the game.

      FAQs:

      1. What happens if the draw pile runs out?
      - Shuffle the discard pile to form a new draw pile.

      2. Can I discard a car part card?
      - Yes, you can discard any card from your hand, including car part cards.

      3. What if multiple players complete their cars at the same time?
      - The player whose turn it is to play announces "Car Complete!" first and wins the game. If it’s not clear, the game ends in a tie.

      4. Can I use more than one of the same part if I have duplicates?
      - No, you can only use one of each part (Engine, Body, Wheels, Interior) to complete a car.

      5. Can I trade cards with other players?
      - No, trading cards is not allowed.

      6. What should I do if I have more than 5 cards at the end of my turn?
      - You must discard until you have exactly 5 cards in your hand at the end of your turn.

      7. Can I play with more than 4 players?
      - Yes, but you may need additional decks for larger groups.

      8. What if I draw a card I can't use?
      - Keep it in your hand or discard it at the end of your turn. You may find it useful in a later turn.

      9. How many cars can I complete in one game?
      - Each player can only complete one car per game. The game ends as soon as any player completes a car.

      10. Are there any special cards in the deck?
      - No, all cards correspond to the car parts needed to complete a car.
