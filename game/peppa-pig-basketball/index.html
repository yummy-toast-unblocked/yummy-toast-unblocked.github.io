      In this game, players will use strategy and quick thinking to complete various challenges and obstacles within a set time limit.

      Instructions:
      1. Gather 2-4 players and decide who will go first.
      2. Shuffle the deck of challenge cards and place them face down in the center.
      3. Each player draws a card on their turn and reads the challenge aloud.
      4. The player has 60 seconds to complete the challenge. Use a timer to keep track.
      5. If the player completes the challenge within the time limit, they keep the card.
      6. If the player fails, the card is placed at the bottom of the deck.
      7. The game continues with the next player drawing a card and attempting a challenge.
      8. The first player to collect 5 cards wins the game.
