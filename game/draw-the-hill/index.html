      Instructions

      Objective: Race your car to the finish line before your opponents.

      Setup:
      1. Place the game board on a flat surface.
      2. Each player chooses a car token and places it at the starting line.
      3. Shuffle the deck of action cards and place it face down.

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      2. On your turn, draw an action card and follow its instructions. Action cards may include:
      - Move Forward: Advance your car the specified number of spaces.
      - Move Backward: Move your car back the specified number of spaces.
      - Boost: Skip ahead to the next checkpoint.
      - Hazard: Lose a turn or move back due to obstacles.
      3. After completing the action, discard the card to a discard pile.

      Winning:
      - The first player to reach the finish line wins the game.
      - If multiple players reach the finish line on the same turn, the player who moved the furthest beyond the finish line wins.

      Additional Rules:
      - If you land on a space occupied by another player, move to the next available space.
      - If the deck of action cards runs out, reshuffle the discard pile to create a new deck.

      Enjoy the race and may the best driver win!
