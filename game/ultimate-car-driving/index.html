      Instructions:
      * Gather a group of players and a deck of cards.
      * Shuffle the deck and deal 5 cards to each player.
      * The remaining cards form the draw pile.
      * The first player begins by playing a card from their hand.
      * Match the card's suit or rank to the card on top of the discard pile.
      * If the player cannot match a card, they must draw a card from the draw pile.
      * The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * What happens if the draw pile runs out?
      * Shuffle the discard pile to create a new draw pile.
      * Can I play a wild card on any card?
      * Yes, a wild card can be played on any card, regardless of suit or rank.
      * Can I pass my turn?
      * No, you cannot pass your turn. If you cannot play a card, you must draw one from the draw pile.
      * What happens if I have more than one card of the same rank?
      * You can play all the cards of the same rank in one turn.
      * Can I look at my opponents' cards?
      * No, you cannot look at your opponents' cards.
