      Instructions

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns drawing cards from a deck.
      2. Each card displays a type of vehicle (e.g., car, truck, motorcycle).
      3. If a player draws a car, they can move their car token one space forward on the board.
      4. If a player draws a truck, they can move their car token two spaces forward.
      5. If a player draws a motorcycle, they can move their car token three spaces forward.
      6. Players can also use special action cards to steal spaces from opponents or skip turns.
      7. The first player to reach the finish line wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can I move my car backward? No.
      * Can I pass other players? Yes.
      * What happens if I land on the same space as another player? Nothing.
      * Can I use multiple special action cards in one turn? Yes.
      * Can I draw multiple cards in a turn? Only if you draw a special action card that allows it.
