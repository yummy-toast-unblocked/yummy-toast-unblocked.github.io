      Instructions:
      * Form two teams of equal size.
      * Determine which team starts with the ball.
      * Players can move the ball by dribbling, passing, or shooting.
      * To score points, a player must shoot the ball through the opponent's basket.
      * The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
      FAQs:
      * How do you move the ball?
      * Dribbling: Bouncing the ball on the ground while running or walking.
      * Passing: Throwing the ball to a teammate.
      * Shooting: Attempting to throw the ball through the basket.
      * How do you score points?
      * Field goal: Worth 2 points if made within the 3-point line, 3 points if made outside the 3-point line.
      * Free throw: Worth 1 point if awarded after a foul.
      * What is a foul?
      * Illegal contact with an opponent, such as charging, blocking, or holding.
      * How does the game end?
      * When time expires.
      * Can you tie the game?
      * Yes, if both teams have the same number of points at the end of regulation time.
      * What happens if the game is tied?
      * A period of overtime is played until one team wins.
