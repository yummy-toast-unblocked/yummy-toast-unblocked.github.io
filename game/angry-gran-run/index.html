      Description

      5.0 (1281 Reviews)

      Getting Over It is a thrilling test of endurance and skill, where you control a man named Diogenes who is wedged inside a cauldron. Armed with only a sledgehammer, you must navigate a bizarre and treacherous landscape, overcoming towering obstacles and mind-bending challenges. Precision, patience, and sheer willpower are your best allies as you make your way to the top. With its punishing difficulty and unpredictable physics, Getting Over It promises a uniquely frustrating yet immensely rewarding experience for those who dare to conquer it.


      Instructions

      In Getting Over It, you control a character in a cauldron using a sledgehammer to climb various obstacles.

      1. Start the game and familiarize yourself with the controls.
      2. Move the mouse to swing the sledgehammer in different directions.
      3. Use the sledgehammer to push or pull yourself over obstacles.
      4. Adjust your movements carefully to maintain balance and avoid falling.
      5. Progress through the game by climbing higher and higher without falling.

      Patience and precision are key to mastering this challenging game.
