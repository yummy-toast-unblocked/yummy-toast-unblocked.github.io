      Instructions

      Objective:
      The objective of the game is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

      Setup:
      1. Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards to each player.
      2. Place the remaining deck face-down in the center to form the draw pile.
      3. Flip the top card of the draw pile and place it next to the pile to begin the discard pile.

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      2. On your turn, you must play a card from your hand that matches the top card of the discard pile by either number, color, or symbol.
      3. If you cannot play a matching card, you must draw one card from the draw pile.
      4. If the drawn card can be played, you may do so immediately. Otherwise, your turn ends.
      5. Special cards have unique effects:
      - Skip: The next player loses their turn.
      - Reverse: Reverses the order of play.
      - Draw Two: The next player draws two cards and loses their turn.
      - Wild: Can be played on any card and allows you to choose a new color.
      - Wild Draw Four: Can be played on any card, allows you to choose a new color, and the next player draws four cards and loses their turn.
      6. When you have only one card left, you must shout "UNO!" to warn other players. If another player catches you not saying "UNO!" before your next turn, you must draw two cards as a penalty.

      Winning:
      The first player to play all their cards wins the game.

      FAQs:

      1. What happens if the draw pile runs out?
      - Shuffle the discard pile to form a new draw pile.

      2. Can you stack Draw Two or Wild Draw Four cards?
      - No, Draw Two and Wild Draw Four cards cannot be stacked.

      3. What if two players shout "UNO!" at the same time?
      - The shout must happen before the next player takes their turn. If both players shout at the same time, the action is valid for both.

      4. Can a Wild Draw Four be played at any time?
      - No, you can only play a Wild Draw Four if you have no other card that matches the color of the top card on the discard pile.

      5. What if a player forgets to draw a card when they can't play?
      - If noticed immediately, the player must draw the card. If the turn has passed, the mistake stands, and play continues.

      6. Can you end the game with a special card?
      - Yes, you can end the game with any card, including special cards.
