      Instructions

      Instructions:

      1. Gather 2-4 players and a deck of 52 playing cards.
      2. Shuffle the deck and deal 13 cards to each player.
      3. The remaining cards form the draw pile.
      4. Players simultaneously sort their cards into suits and ranks.
      5. On your turn, ask another player for a specific card. If they have it, they must give it to you.
      6. If the player you ask does not have the card, draw from the draw pile.
      7. If you draw the card you asked for, you can immediately ask another player for a card.
      8. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.

      FAQs:

      Q: Can I ask for any card?
      A: Yes, you can ask for any card that you need to complete a set or build a run.

      Q: What happens if I draw a card that I don't need?
      A: You can discard it or keep it in your hand.

      Q: What is a run?
      A: A run is a set of three or more cards in sequence, regardless of suit.

      Q: What is a set?
      A: A set is a group of three or more cards of the same rank.
