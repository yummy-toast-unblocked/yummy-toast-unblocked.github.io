      Water Sort

      Description

      5.0 (7914 Reviews)

      Dive into the tranquil world of Water Sort, a captivating puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. Arrange vibrant liquids into their respective containers, ensuring each hue finds its perfect match. With each level, the complexity increases, requiring keen observation and clever manipulation to achieve harmony. Can you master the art of sorting and bring order to chaos? Experience the soothing satisfaction of perfectly aligned colors in Water Sort.


      Instructions

      Objective: Sort the colored water in the bottles so that each bottle contains only one color.

      1. Tap on a bottle to select it.
      2. Tap on another bottle to pour the top layer of water into the selected bottle.
      3. You can only pour water if the top color of both bottles is the same or if the receiving bottle is empty.
      4. Continue pouring until all bottles contain water of only one color.
      5. If you make a mistake, use the undo button to revert your last move.
      6. Use the hint button if you need help figuring out your next move.
      7. Complete the level to progress to the next, more challenging stage.

      Tip: Plan your moves ahead to avoid getting stuck!
