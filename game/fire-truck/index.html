      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Each player chooses a car and places it on the starting line.
      * Roll a die to determine which car moves first.
      * Roll the die again to move your car forward the number of spaces indicated.
      * If you land on a special space, follow the instructions on that space.
      * The first player to reach the finish line wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can you move backward? No.
      * What happens if you roll a number that would take you past the finish line? You lose your turn.
      * What do the special spaces do? They can award extra moves, skip turns, or send you back spaces.
      * Can you trade cars with other players? Yes, but only with their permission.
      * Can you have more than one car on a space? No, only one car can occupy a space at a time.
