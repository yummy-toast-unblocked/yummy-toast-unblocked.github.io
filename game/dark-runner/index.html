      Instructions

      In this game, players take on the role of explorers uncovering hidden treasures and ancient artifacts. The aim is to gather as many treasures as possible while avoiding traps and obstacles within a set number of turns. The game is suitable for 2-4 players and typically lasts 30-45 minutes.

      Setup:


      1. Place the game board in the center of the table.
      2. Shuffle the treasure and trap cards separately and place them in two distinct piles.
      3. Each player selects a token and places it at the starting point on the board.
      4. Distribute an equal number of action cards to each player.

      Playing the Game:


      1. Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      2. On your turn, draw an action card and follow its instructions.
      3. Move your token according to the number or directions indicated on the action card.
      4. If you land on a treasure spot, draw a treasure card and keep it.
      5. If you land on a trap spot, draw a trap card and follow its instructions.
      6. Use strategy to avoid traps and collect treasures efficiently.
      7. The game ends when all treasure cards have been collected or all players have used their turns.

      Winning the Game:


      - The player with the most treasures at the end of the game wins.

      FAQs:

      Q: Can I skip a turn?
      A: No, players must take a turn each round.

      Q: What happens if I draw a trap card?
      A: Follow the instructions on the trap card, which may include losing a turn or moving your token back a few spaces.

      Q: Can I trade treasures with other players?
      A: No, trading treasures is not allowed in this game.

      Q: What if two players have the same number of treasures at the end?
      A: The player with the highest value of treasures wins. If it’s still a tie, both players share the victory.

      Q: Are there any bonus cards?
      A: Yes, some action cards provide bonuses such as extra turns or immunity from traps for one round.

      Q: Can I use more than one action card per turn?
      A: No, you can only use one action card per turn.

      Q: What if I run out of action cards?
      A: Reshuffle the used action cards to create a new draw pile.

      Q: Is there a time limit for each turn?
      A: No, but players are encouraged to play promptly to keep the game moving.

      Enjoy your adventure and may you uncover the greatest treasures!
