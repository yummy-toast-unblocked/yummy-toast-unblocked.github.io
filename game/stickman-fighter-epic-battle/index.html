      Instructions

      Instructions:
      1. Gather players (2 or more) and a deck of cards.
      2. Remove all face cards and jokers.
      3. Shuffle the deck and deal five cards to each player.
      4. Place the remaining deck face down in the center of the table.
      5. The player to the left of the dealer goes first.
      6. On your turn, you may play one or more cards from your hand face up in front of you.
      7. Cards can be played in sets of three or more with the same number or in a run of three or more cards of the same suit.
      8. If you cannot play any cards, you must draw a card from the deck.
      9. If the deck is empty, you must pass your turn.
      10. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can I play a set and a run in the same turn?
      * Yes.
      * Can I play a card that is already on the table?
      * Yes, if it completes a set or run.
      * What happens if I draw a card and it completes a set or run for another player?
      * The other player can play the set or run immediately.
      * Can I play a card that is part of a set or run that I have already played?
      * No.
