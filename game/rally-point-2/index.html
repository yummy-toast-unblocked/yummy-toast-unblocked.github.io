      Instructions

      Instructions:

      1. Objective: Reach the finish line as quickly as possible while avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups.

      2. Controls:
      - Use the arrow keys:
      - Up arrow to accelerate
      - Down arrow to brake/reverse
      - Left arrow to steer left
      - Right arrow to steer right
      - Press the spacebar to use a power-up.

      3. Gameplay:
      - Start by selecting a car and a track.
      - Navigate through the track, avoiding obstacles like other cars, barriers, and oil spills.
      - Collect power-ups such as speed boosts, shields, and repair kits to gain advantages.
      - Complete the race in the shortest time possible to win.

      4. Winning:
      - The player with the fastest time at the end of the race wins.
      - If playing in multiplayer mode, the first player to cross the finish line wins.

      FAQs:

      1. How do I start the game?
      - Select a car and track from the main menu, then press the start button.

      2. What are the controls for the game?
      - Use the arrow keys for navigation and the spacebar to use power-ups.

      3. How do I use power-ups?
      - Press the spacebar to activate a collected power-up.

      4. What happens if I hit an obstacle?
      - Hitting obstacles will slow you down and may cause damage to your car.

      5. Can I play with friends?
      - Yes, the game has a multiplayer mode for racing against friends.

      6. How do I win the game?
      - Complete the race in the shortest time or be the first to cross the finish line in multiplayer mode.

      7. What types of power-ups are available?
      - Power-ups include speed boosts, shields, and repair kits.

      8. Can I customize my car?
      - Yes, you can customize your car's appearance and performance from the garage menu.

      9. What do I do if my car gets damaged?
      - Collect repair kits to restore your car's health during the race.

      10. Are there different difficulty levels?
      - Yes, you can choose from various difficulty levels before starting the race.

      11. Is there a time limit for each race?
      - No, but your goal is to complete the race as quickly as possible to achieve the best time.

      12. What should I do if I get stuck?
      - If you get stuck, try reversing or steering around the obstacle. If that doesn't work, restart the race from the pause menu.
