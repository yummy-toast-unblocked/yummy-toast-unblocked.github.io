      Instructions

      Gameplay
      1. Roll the dice to move your car around the board.
      2. Land on spaces to collect money, buy properties, or pay rent.
      3. Build houses and hotels on your properties to increase rent.
      4. Bankrupt other players by forcing them to pay more rent than they can afford.
      5. The last player with money wins.
      FAQs
      * What happens if you land on an unowned property?
      * You can buy it from the bank or auction it off to other players.
      * What happens if you land on a property owned by another player?
      * You have to pay them rent.
      * How do you build houses and hotels?
      * You need to own all the properties in a color group and have enough money.
      * What happens if you run out of money?
      * You can mortgage your properties or declare bankruptcy.
