      Instructions

      Objective:
      The goal of the game is to successfully complete a series of challenges that test your skills in various areas, such as coordination, strategy, and problem-solving.

      Setup:
      1. Gather all game components: challenge cards, timer, scoring tokens, and any necessary tools (e.g., dice, spinners).
      2. Shuffle the challenge cards and place them in a stack face-down.
      3. Each player selects a token to represent their score.

      Gameplay:
      1. The youngest player starts, and play continues clockwise.
      2. On your turn, draw a challenge card from the top of the stack.
      3. Read the challenge out loud and set the timer according to the card's instructions.
      4. Attempt to complete the challenge within the given time.
      5. If you succeed, take a scoring token. If you fail, return the card to the bottom of the stack.
      6. Pass the turn to the next player.
      7. Continue playing until all challenge cards have been attempted or a predetermined number of rounds have been completed.

      Winning:
      The player with the most scoring tokens at the end of the game is the winner.

      FAQs:

      Q: What happens if there is a tie at the end of the game?
      A: In the event of a tie, the tied players each draw one additional challenge card and attempt the challenge. The player who completes their challenge successfully first wins.

      Q: Can players help each other during challenges?
      A: No, players must complete challenges on their own without assistance from others.

      Q: What if a player cannot attempt a challenge due to a physical limitation?
      A: If a player cannot attempt a challenge due to a physical limitation, they may draw a new challenge card without penalty.

      Q: Can we modify the time limit for challenges?
      A: Yes, players can agree to modify the time limit for challenges, but it should be consistent for all players.

      Q: Are there any penalties for failing a challenge?
      A: There are no penalties for failing a challenge, but the player does not earn a scoring token for that round.

      Q: What if a challenge card is unclear or ambiguous?
      A: If a challenge card is unclear or ambiguous, players should discuss and agree on a fair interpretation before the challenge is attempted.

      Q: Can we create our own challenges?
      A: Yes, players can create their own challenges to add variety to the game, but all players must agree on the new challenges before they are added.
