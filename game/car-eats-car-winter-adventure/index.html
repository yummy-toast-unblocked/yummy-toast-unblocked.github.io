      Instructions

      Drift Hunters is a car drifting game where you can earn points and customize your vehicle. Follow these instructions to play:

      1. Choose Your Car: Start by selecting a car from the available options.
      2. Customize Your Vehicle: Use earned points to upgrade parts like engine, brakes, and tires. You can also change the car's appearance.
      3. Select a Track: Pick a track to start drifting. Different tracks offer varying levels of difficulty.
      4. Drifting Controls:
      - Accelerate: Press the up arrow key or W.
      - Brake/Reverse: Press the down arrow key or S.
      - Steer Left: Press the left arrow key or A.
      - Steer Right: Press the right arrow key or D.
      - Handbrake: Press the spacebar for sharper drifts.
      5. Earn Points: Drift around corners to earn points. The longer and more controlled the drift, the more points you accumulate.
      6. Bank Your Score: Complete a lap to bank your score. Use these points to further customize and upgrade your car.
      7. Repeat and Improve: Keep practicing on different tracks to improve your drifting skills and earn more points for upgrades.

      Enjoy drifting and aim for the highest score!
