      Gameplay Instructions:
      * Control your player with the arrow keys or analog stick.
      * Press the spacebar or A button to jump.
      * Dunk or shoot by pressing the E or B button while in the air near the basket.
      * Block shots by pressing the F or X button while near an opponent.
      * Steal the ball by pressing the G or Y button while near an opponent with the ball.
      * Pass the ball by pressing the Q or L button while facing a teammate.
