      Instructions:
      1. Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards to each player.
      2. Place the remaining deck face down in the center as the draw pile.
      3. Flip the top card of the draw pile face up to start the discard pile.
      4. On your turn, you must either:
      - Play a card from your hand that matches the suit or number of the top card on the discard pile.
      - Draw a card from the draw pile.
      5. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * What happens if I can't play a card? You must draw a card from the draw pile.
      * Can I play a card that matches either the suit or number of the top card? Yes, you can play either.
      * What happens if I draw the same card that's on top of the discard pile? You can play it immediately.
      * Can I look at the bottom card of the draw pile? No, the bottom card is not visible to players.
      * What happens if the draw pile runs out? Shuffle the discard pile and place it face down as the new draw pile.
