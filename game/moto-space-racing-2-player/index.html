      Instructions

      Objective: Be the last kart standing by collecting weapons and power-ups to eliminate opponents.

      1. Start the Game: Choose your kart and character.
      2. Controls:
      - WASD or Arrow Keys to move.
      - Spacebar to use items.
      3. Collect Items: Drive over item boxes to get weapons and power-ups.
      4. Attack Opponents: Use collected weapons to damage other karts.
      5. Avoid Damage: Dodge incoming attacks and use power-ups for defense.
      6. Health Bar: Monitor your health bar; when it depletes, you are out of the game.
      7. Winning: The last remaining kart wins the match.

      Tips:
      - Stay Mobile: Keep moving to avoid becoming an easy target.
      - Strategize: Use different weapons strategically based on the situation.
