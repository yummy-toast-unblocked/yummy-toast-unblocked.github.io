      Instructions:

      1. Setup:
      - Each player selects a character or avatar.
      - Place the game board on a flat surface.
      - Shuffle the deck of cards and place them face down in the designated area.

      2. Starting the Game:
      - Determine the starting player by rolling a die or any agreed method.
      - Each player draws 3 cards from the deck.

      3. Game Play:
      - Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      - On your turn, you may play one card from your hand, move your character according to the card's instructions, and draw a new card.
      - Follow the specific actions or effects indicated on the cards.

      4. Winning the Game:
      - The game ends when a player successfully reaches the final goal or accumulates the required number of points.
      - The first player to reach the goal or the player with the highest points when the deck is exhausted wins the game.

      FAQs:

      1. Q: How many players can play?
      - A: The game supports 2 to 6 players.

      2. Q: What happens if I run out of cards in my hand?
      - A: Draw 3 new cards from the deck immediately.

      3. Q: Can I skip my turn?
      - A: No, each player must take their turn and follow the game rules.

      4. Q: What if I draw a card that I cannot use?
      - A: You can choose to discard it and draw a new card.

      5. Q: How long does a typical game last?
      - A: A typical game lasts about 30 to 45 minutes, depending on the number of players.

      6. Q: Can we modify the rules?
      - A: Yes, players can agree on house rules before starting the game.

      7. Q: What if there is a tie at the end of the game?
      - A: In the case of a tie, the game continues until one player surpasses the others in points or reaches the goal first.
