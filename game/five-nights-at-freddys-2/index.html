      Instructions

      In this action-packed game, players navigate through various levels filled with enemies, obstacles, and challenges. Your objective is to defeat adversaries, collect power-ups, and reach the end of each stage.

      Instructions:
      1. Start the Game: Press 'Start' on the main menu to begin your adventure.
      2. Movement: Use the arrow keys (or joystick) to move your character left, right, up, and down.
      3. Attacking: Press the 'A' button to perform an attack. You can execute different types of attacks by combining movement directions with the attack button.
      4. Jumping: Press the 'B' button to jump. Combine with movement keys for longer or higher jumps.
      5. Special Abilities: Use the 'C' button to activate special abilities. These abilities consume energy or resources, so use them wisely.
      6. Collect Items: Walk over items to pick them up. Collect health packs, power-ups, and keys to aid your progress.
      7. Pause/Inventory: Press the 'Start' button to pause the game and access your inventory. Here you can check items, view the map, and equip or use items.
      8. Defeat Bosses: Each level ends with a boss fight. Learn their patterns and use your skills to defeat them.
      9. Complete Levels: Reach the end of each level to progress to the next. Your score and remaining health will be tallied at the end of each stage.

      FAQs:

      Q: How do I save my progress?
      A: The game automatically saves your progress at the end of each level. You can also manually save from the pause menu.

      Q: What if I run out of health?
      A: If you run out of health, you will respawn at the last checkpoint. You may lose some progress, but items collected remain with you.

      Q: Are there different difficulty settings?
      A: Yes, you can choose between Easy, Normal, and Hard modes at the start of the game or from the options menu.

      Q: Can I change the controls?
      A: Yes, you can customize the control scheme from the settings menu to suit your preferences.

      Q: How do I use power-ups?
      A: Power-ups are automatically activated upon collection. Some power-ups provide temporary boosts, while others are added to your inventory for later use.

      Q: What do I do if I get stuck?
      A: Use the map in your inventory to find clues about where to go next. Additionally, talking to NPCs and reading in-game hints can provide guidance.

      Q: Are there multiplayer options?
      A: The game features a cooperative multiplayer mode where you can play with a friend. Access it from the main menu under 'Multiplayer'.

      Q: How can I unlock new characters or abilities?
      A: New characters and abilities are unlocked as you progress through the game. Completing specific challenges and finding hidden items will also unlock additional content.

      Q: Is there a way to replay levels?
      A: Yes, you can replay completed levels from the level select screen in the main menu to improve your score or find missed items.

      Q: What should I do if I encounter a bug or glitch?
      A: Please report any bugs or glitches through the feedback option in the main menu. Your reports help us improve the game.
