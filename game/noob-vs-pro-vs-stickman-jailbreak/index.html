      Instructions

      Gameplay:
      1. Join a team of two or three players.
      2. Navigate through a maze-like arena, collecting power-ups and avoiding traps.
      3. Capture the opposing team's flag and bring it back to your base.
      4. Eliminate enemy players with weapons and abilities.
      5. Repeat steps 2-4 until one team captures the required number of flags or the time runs out.
      FAQs:
      * What are the different power-ups?
      * Speed boost: Increases player movement speed.
      * Shield: Blocks enemy attacks for a short duration.
      * Health pack: Restores player health.
      * Invisibility: Makes the player temporarily invisible to enemies.
      * What are the different weapons?
      * Blaster: Fires projectiles that deal damage.
      * Shotgun: Fires a spread of projectiles at close range.
      * Rocket launcher: Fires rockets that deal splash damage.
      * How do I capture the enemy's flag?
      * Touch the flag and hold the "Capture" button for a few seconds.
      * What happens when I'm eliminated?
      * You will respawn at your base after a short time.
      * How do I win the game?
      * Capture the required number of flags (usually 3) before the opposing team or before the time runs out.
