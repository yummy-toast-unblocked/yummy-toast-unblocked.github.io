      Instructions:
      1. Choose a driver and passengers.
      2. Seat everyone in the car.
      3. One person calls out "Car!" and everyone starts making car noises and gestures.
      4. The last person to make a car noise or gesture is out.
      5. Continue playing until only one person remains.
      FAQs:
      * What kind of car noises and gestures are allowed?
      - Any noises or gestures related to cars, such as revving engines, honking horns, or steering.
      * Can I use my imagination to make up new car noises or gestures?
      - Yes, as long as they are recognizable as car-related.
      * What happens if the driver makes a noise or gesture after everyone else is out?
      - The driver is also out.
      * Can I play with more than one car?
      - Yes, but each car should have its own driver and passengers.
