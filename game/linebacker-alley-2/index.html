      Instructions:
      * Divide players into two teams of equal size.
      * Set up a playing field with a goal at each end.
      * Kick or throw the ball to start the game.
      * Players attempt to score points by carrying or passing the ball into the opposing team's goal.
      * The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
      FAQs:
      * What happens if a player touches the ball out of bounds? The ball is awarded to the opposing team.
      * What is a foul? A foul occurs when a player illegally obstructs or interferes with an opponent.
      * What is a penalty kick? A penalty kick is awarded to the team that was fouled and is taken from a designated spot on the field.
      * What is a corner kick? A corner kick is awarded when the ball goes out of bounds over the goal line and the last player to touch it was from the defending team.
      * What is a free kick? A free kick is awarded for certain fouls and is taken from the spot where the foul occurred.
