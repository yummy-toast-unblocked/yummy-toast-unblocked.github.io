      Instructions

      Gameplay:
      1. Divide into two teams of two players each.
      2. Each team takes turns placing one of their pieces on an empty square of the 5x5 grid.
      3. Place pieces to create a line of three or more in a row, column, or diagonal.
      4. If a team creates a line, they remove all opponent pieces in that line.
      5. The first team to remove all of their opponent's pieces wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can I move my pieces after they are placed? No, once a piece is placed, it cannot be moved.
      * Can I place a piece on top of an opponent's piece? No, you can only place a piece on an empty square.
      * What happens if the game ends in a draw? The game is a draw if neither team can create a line of three or more pieces.
      * Can I place pieces diagonally? Yes, you can place pieces diagonally, but they must still form a line of three or more.
      * Can I place pieces on the edges of the grid? Yes, you can place pieces on the edges of the grid, but they must still be part of a line of three or more.
