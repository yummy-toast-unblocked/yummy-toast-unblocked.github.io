      Among Us

      Description

      5.0 (842 Reviews)

      Among Us is an exciting multiplayer game where you join a crew of astronauts on a spaceship, space station, or planetary base. Your mission is to complete tasks to maintain the ship, but beware—impostors lurk among you, aiming to sabotage the mission and eliminate the crew. With its blend of strategy, deception, and social interaction, Among Us challenges you to deduce who the impostors are before it's too late. Engage in intense discussions, vote out suspects, and use your wits to survive in this thrilling game of trust and treachery.


      Instructions

      Among Us is a multiplayer game where players take on the roles of Crewmates or Impostors on a spaceship. The Crewmates aim to complete all tasks or identify the Impostors. The Impostors aim to eliminate Crewmates and sabotage the mission.

      Setup:
      1. Download and install the Among Us game on your device.
      2. Create or join a lobby with other players.
      3. Customize your character with different colors and accessories.

      Gameplay:
      1. At the start, you will be assigned a role: Crewmate or Impostor.
      2. Crewmates:
      - Complete assigned tasks displayed on the screen.
      - Report any dead bodies you find.
      - Call emergency meetings if you suspect someone.
      3. Impostors:
      - Blend in with the Crewmates.
      - Sabotage systems to cause chaos and create opportunities.
      - Eliminate Crewmates without getting caught.

      Meetings and Voting:
      1. During meetings, discuss who you believe the Impostors are.
      2. Players will vote to eject a suspect or skip the vote.
      3. If a player is ejected, their role is revealed.

      Winning:
      - Crewmates win by completing all tasks or successfully ejecting all Impostors.
      - Impostors win by eliminating enough Crewmates so the number of Impostors equals the number of Crewmates or through successful sabotage.

      Tips:
      - Use security cameras and admin maps to monitor activity.
      - Communicate effectively and be observant during meetings.
      - If you're an Impostor, be strategic about your actions and alibis.
