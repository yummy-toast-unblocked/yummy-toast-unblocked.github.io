      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Objective: Protect your base from waves of enemy units.
      * Controls:
      * Use the mouse to aim and shoot.
      * Click towers to upgrade or repair them.
      * Drag towers from the bottom panel to place them on the map.
      * Gameplay:
      * Enemy units will spawn from the left and right sides of the map.
      * Towers automatically attack enemy units within range.
      * Upgrade towers to increase their range, damage, and attack speed.
      * Use abilities to support your towers or damage enemies.
      * Survive as many waves as possible.
      FAQs:
      * How do I earn money?
      * You earn money by killing enemy units.
      * What happens if I lose?
      * You will have to restart the current level.
      * How do I unlock new towers?
      * You can unlock new towers by completing levels and earning experience.
      * What are abilities?
      * Abilities are special powers that can be used to support your towers or damage enemies.
      * How do I upgrade towers?
      * Click on a tower and click the "Upgrade" button.
