      Instructions

      Galaga is a classic arcade shooter game where you control a spaceship at the bottom of the screen. Your goal is to destroy waves of enemy aliens.

      1. Start the Game: Insert a coin (if applicable) and press the Start button.
      2. Move Your Ship: Use the joystick or arrow keys to move your spaceship left and right.
      3. Fire: Press the fire button to shoot lasers at the enemy aliens.
      4. Avoid Enemy Fire: Dodge incoming shots from the aliens to avoid losing a life.
      5. Capture and Rescue: Some aliens will try to capture your ship with a tractor beam. If captured, you can rescue it by destroying the capturing alien, which will allow you to control two ships simultaneously for double firepower.
      6. Bonus Stages: In bonus stages, shoot as many aliens as possible for extra points without the risk of losing a life.
      7. Advance Levels: Each wave of enemies defeated will advance you to the next level, where the difficulty increases.
      8. Game Over: The game ends when all your lives are lost.

      Scoring: Destroy enemies to earn points. The more enemies you destroy, the higher your score.
