      FAQs:

      1. How do I save my game?
      - The game automatically saves at checkpoints. Ensure you reach these points to save your progress.

      2. What happens if I lose all my lives?
      - If you lose all your lives, you will restart from the last checkpoint or the beginning of the level.

      3. How do I unlock new abilities?
      - Earn experience points (XP) by completing missions and defeating enemies. Use XP to unlock new abilities in the abilities menu.

      4. Can I replay levels to find missed items?
      - Yes, you can replay any level to find missed items or complete side missions.

      5. Is there a multiplayer mode?
      - Currently, the game offers a single-player experience only.

      6. How do I interact with NPCs?
      - Approach an NPC and press the 'E' key to interact and receive missions or information.

      7. Are there difficulty settings?
      - The game features adjustable difficulty settings in the options menu. Choose from easy, medium, or hard.

      8. What types of power-ups are available?
      - Power-ups include health boosts, temporary invincibility, increased attack power, and speed boosts.

      9. How can I view my current mission objectives?
      - Press the 'M' key or access the missions menu to view your current objectives.

      10. How do I defeat bosses?
      - Bosses require a combination of basic attacks, special abilities, and strategic movement. Observe their patterns and exploit their weaknesses.

      11. Can I customize my character?
