      Instructions

      Gameplay:
      1. Deal 5 cards to each player.
      2. Place the rest of the deck in a pile in the center.
      3. Flip the top card of the deck and place it face up next to the deck.
      4. On your turn, you can play a card from your hand that matches either the suit or number of the top card on the discard pile.
      5. If you can't play a card, draw one from the deck.
      6. If you draw a card that you can play, play it immediately.
      7. If you draw a card that you can't play, your turn ends.
      8. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * What happens if I can't play a card and the deck is empty? You lose your turn.
      * Can I play multiple cards in a row if they all match the top card? Yes.
      * What happens if I play a card that doesn't match the top card? Your turn ends and you draw a penalty card.
      * Can I look at the cards in the deck? No.
      * Can I change the order of my cards in my hand? No.
