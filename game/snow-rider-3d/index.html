      Instructions

      Objective: Make strategic moves to navigate the board, collect items, and achieve specific goals within the game.

      Setup:
      1. Place the game board on a flat surface.
      2. Distribute player tokens to each participant.
      3. Shuffle and place the item and goal cards in their respective decks.
      4. Each player draws a starting hand of 3 item cards.

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      2. On your turn, you can perform up to 3 actions:
      a. Move: Advance your token up to 3 spaces in any direction.
      b. Draw: Pick one item card from the deck.
      c. Use: Play an item card from your hand to gain an advantage.
      3. Players must navigate the board to collect items and achieve goals specified by their goal cards.
      4. The game continues until a player completes all their goals or the item deck is exhausted.

      Winning:
      - The first player to complete all their goal cards wins.
      - If the item deck is exhausted, the player with the most goals completed wins.

      FAQs:

      Q: How many players can participate?
      A: The game is designed for 2-4 players.

      Q: What happens if two players complete their goals on the same turn?
      A: The player who completed their goals first in the turn order wins.

      Q: Can I move diagonally on the board?
      A: No, you can only move horizontally or vertically.

      Q: What if I draw an item card that I can't use?
      A: You can choose to keep it in your hand for future turns or discard it to draw a new one on your next turn.

      Q: Are there any penalties for running out of item cards in your hand?
      A: No, you can always draw a new item card on your turn if you have fewer than 3 in your hand.

      Q: Can I skip my turn if I don't want to perform any actions?
      A: Yes, you can choose to skip your turn if you prefer.

      Q: How do I know what my goals are?
      A: Your goal cards will specify what you need to achieve, such as collecting specific items or reaching certain spaces on the board.

      Q: Can I block other players from moving?
      A: No, player tokens cannot occupy the same space, but you cannot intentionally block others.

      Q: Is there a time limit for each turn?
      A: No, but players are encouraged to keep the game moving by making decisions promptly.
