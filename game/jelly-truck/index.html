      Instructions

      1. Gather 2-4 players and sit in a circle.
      2. Each player receives 5 skill cards, which range in value from 1 to 10.
      3. Shuffle the skill deck and place it face down in the center.
      4. Draw the top card from the skill deck and place it face up next to the deck to start the play pile.
      5. On your turn, play one card from your hand onto the play pile. The card must have a higher value than the top card on the pile.
      6. If you cannot play a higher card, draw a card from the skill deck.
      7. When a player successfully plays a card, the turn passes to the next player clockwise.
      8. The first player to play all their cards wins the game.
      9. Additional rules: If a player draws a card and it's a special action card (e.g., reverse, skip, or draw 2), follow the instructions on the card and then proceed with the game.

      Have fun and use strategic thinking to outplay your opponents!
