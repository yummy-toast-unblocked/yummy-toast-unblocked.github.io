      Instructions

      In this action-packed game, players are tasked with navigating various levels filled with enemies, obstacles, and hidden treasures. Your objective is to defeat enemies, collect items, and reach the end of each level while maximizing your score.

      How to Play:

      1. Movement:
      - Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to move your character.
      - Press the "Jump" key (Spacebar) to leap over obstacles or reach higher platforms.

      2. Combat:
      - Use the "Attack" button (Ctrl or Left Mouse Button) to strike enemies.
      - Hold the "Block" button (Shift or Right Mouse Button) to defend against enemy attacks.

      3. Special Abilities:
      - Press the "Ability" key (E or Q) to use your character's special power. Each ability has a cooldown period.

      4. Collecting Items:
      - Move over items to pick them up. Collect health packs to restore health and coins to increase your score.

      5. Objective:
      - Reach the end of each level by navigating through obstacles and defeating enemies. Achieve the highest score possible by collecting items and finishing levels quickly.

      FAQs:

      1. How do I save my progress?
      - The game auto-saves at the end of each level and at designated checkpoints within levels.

      2. Can I change the control settings?
      - Yes, go to the "Settings" menu and select "Controls" to customize your key bindings.

      3. What happens if I lose all my health?
      - If you lose all your health, you will respawn at the last checkpoint. If you haven't reached a checkpoint, you will restart the level.

      4. How do I unlock new abilities?
      - New abilities are unlocked as you progress through the game. Some abilities are found in hidden areas, so explore each level thoroughly.

      5. Are there different difficulty levels?
      - Yes, you can choose from Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulties in the "Settings" menu before starting a new game.

      6. Can I play this game with a controller?
      - Yes, the game supports most standard game controllers. Plug in your controller and configure it in the "Settings" menu.

      7. What should I do if I encounter a bug?
      - If you encounter a bug, please report it through the "Feedback" option in the main menu, including a description of the issue and any relevant screenshots.

      8. Is there a multiplayer mode?
      - Currently, the game is single-player only, but a multiplayer mode is planned for a future update.
