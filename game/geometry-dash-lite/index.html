      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Drag and drop blocks to create horizontal or vertical lines of three or more blocks of the same color.
      * Blocks can fall from above, so plan ahead.
      * Clear lines to score points and create space for more blocks.
      * The game ends when the stack of blocks reaches the top of the screen.
      FAQs:
      * Can I rotate blocks? No, blocks cannot be rotated.
      * How do I get rid of unwanted blocks? You can use power-ups like bombs or rockets to eliminate them.
      * What are the different power-ups? Bombs destroy surrounding blocks, rockets clear entire lines, and color changers transform blocks into any color.
      * How do I pause the game? Press the "Pause" button in the upper-right corner.
      * Can I play with friends? No, this is a single-player game.
