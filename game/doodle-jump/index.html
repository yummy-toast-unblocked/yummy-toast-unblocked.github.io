      Instructions:
      - Guide the falling shapes into place to create horizontal lines without gaps.
      - Lines completed at the top of the screen disappear, making room for more shapes.
      - The game ends when shapes pile up too high and reach the top.
      - Use the arrow keys or WASD to move and rotate the shapes.
      - Spacebar to drop the shapes faster.
