      Race against opponents to reach the finish line first.

      How to Play:
      1. Select your vehicle from the available options.
      2. Choose a track to race on.
      3. Use the arrow keys or your controller to steer your vehicle:
      - Up Arrow / Forward Button: Accelerate
      - Down Arrow / Backward Button: Brake/Reverse
      - Left Arrow / Left Button: Turn Left
      - Right Arrow / Right Button: Turn Right
      4. Press the Boost button (usually Spacebar or a specific controller button) to use a temporary speed increase.
      5. Avoid obstacles and other vehicles to maintain your speed.
