      Instructions

      Instructions:
      1. Gather two or more players and form two teams of equal size.
      2. Designate a starting point and a finish line.
      3. One team lines up at the starting point, facing the finish line. The other team chases them.
      4. The runners from the first team try to reach the finish line without being tagged by the chasers.
      5. If a runner is tagged, they become a member of the chasing team.
      6. The game continues until all runners from one team have been tagged.
      FAQs:
      * Can runners block or tackle the chasers? No, runners can only run to avoid being tagged.
      * What happens if a runner goes out of bounds? They must return to the boundary line and start running again.
      * Can the chasing team split up to cover more ground? Yes, they can divide into groups to search for runners.
      * What if the game ends in a tie? The teams can agree on a rematch or declare a draw.
