      Instructions

      Objective:
      Navigate your bike through various levels, avoiding obstacles and collecting points by performing stunts.

      Controls:
      - Arrow Keys: Control the bike’s movement.
      - Up Arrow: Accelerate
      - Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
      - Left Arrow: Lean Back
      - Right Arrow: Lean Forward
      - Spacebar: Jump
      - R Key: Restart Level

      Gameplay:
      1. Start the game by selecting a level.
      2. Use the arrow keys to control your bike and navigate the course.
      3. Perform stunts by using a combination of jumps (Spacebar) and leaning (Left/Right Arrow) to earn extra points.
      4. Avoid crashing into obstacles to prevent losing points or having to restart the level.
      5. Reach the finish line to complete the level and move on to the next one.

      FAQs:

      Q: How do I unlock new levels?
      A: New levels are unlocked by completing the previous levels. Finish a level to unlock the next one.

      Q: What happens if I crash my bike?
      A: Crashing will cause you to lose points and restart from the last checkpoint or the beginning of the level.

      Q: How can I perform stunts?
      A: Use the Spacebar to jump and combine it with the Left/Right Arrow keys to perform flips and other stunts in the air. Successfully landing these stunts will earn you extra points.

      Q: Can I customize my bike?
      A: Yes, you can customize your bike in the garage section. Use the points you collect to purchase new parts and designs.

      Q: What do I do if I get stuck on a level?
      A: If you get stuck, you can press the R key to restart the level and try a different strategy.

      Q: Is there a multiplayer mode?
      A: The game currently does not support multiplayer mode. It’s designed for single-player enjoyment.

      Q: How do I save my progress?
      A: The game automatically saves your progress at the end of each level. You can continue from where you left off the next time you play.

      Q: Are there any in-game purchases?
      A: The game offers optional in-game purchases for additional customization options and power-ups. These can be accessed through the in-game store.

      Q: How do I earn points?
      A: Points are earned by completing levels, performing stunts, and collecting in-game items scattered throughout the course.

      Q: Is there a way to adjust the difficulty?
      A: Yes, you can adjust the difficulty settings in the game options menu to make the game easier or more challenging based on your preference.
