      Instructions

      Objective: Pot all the balls into the pockets.

      1. Aim the cue towards the ball you want to hit.
      2. Click and drag the cue to set the power and direction of your shot.
      3. Release the mouse button to take the shot.
      4. Pot the balls by hitting them into the pockets.
      5. Avoid scratching by not potting the cue ball or hitting the wrong balls.
      6. Clear all the balls in the least number of shots to win.

      Tip: Use the angles of the table sides to your advantage for better shots.
