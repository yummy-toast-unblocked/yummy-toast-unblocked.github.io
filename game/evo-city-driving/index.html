      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Each player chooses a car and places it on "Start".
      * Roll the dice and move your car forward the number of spaces indicated.
      * Land on a red space to draw a card from the "Action" pile.
      * Follow the instructions on the card.
      * Land on a green space to draw a card from the "Bonus" pile.
      * Be the first player to reach "Finish" to win.
      FAQs:
      * What happens if I land on the same space as another player?
      * Nothing happens. You can both occupy the same space.
      * Can I move backwards?
      * No, you can only move forward.
      * What do I do if I roll a number that would take me past "Finish"?
      * Do not move. Stay on the space before "Finish" until your next turn.
      * Can I trade spaces with another player?
      * No, trading spaces is not allowed.
      * What happens if I draw an "Action" card that says "Go back 3 spaces"?
      * Move your car back 3 spaces, even if it takes you past "Start".
