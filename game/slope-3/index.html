      1. Setup: Place the game board on a flat surface and distribute the game pieces to each player.

      2. Objective: The goal is to be the first player to navigate all your game pieces from the starting area to the designated finish zone.

      3. Turn Sequence:
      - Each player takes turns rolling the dice.
      - Move one of your game pieces forward the number of spaces indicated by the dice roll.
      - If you land on a special space, follow the instructions for that space (e.g., move forward, move backward, lose a turn).

      4. Obstacles: Some spaces may have obstacles or challenges that require you to perform a specific skill (e.g., solve a puzzle, balance an object, or complete a physical task).
