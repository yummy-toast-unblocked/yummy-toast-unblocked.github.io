      Instructions

      Objective:
      In "", the goal is to complete various skill-based challenges to earn points. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

      Setup:
      1. Each player selects a game piece and places it at the starting position on the game board.
      2. Shuffle the Challenge Cards and place them face down in the center of the board.
      3. Place the Timer and Scorepad nearby.

      Gameplay:
      1. The youngest player starts the game.
      2. On your turn, draw the top Challenge Card and read it aloud.
      3. Start the Timer and complete the challenge described on the card.
      4. If you complete the challenge within the allotted time, you earn the points indicated on the card. Record the points on the Scorepad.
      5. If you fail to complete the challenge, you earn no points for that round.
      6. Pass the Timer and Challenge Deck to the next player.
      7. Continue playing until all players have had an equal number of turns or the Challenge Deck is exhausted.

      Winning:
      - The player with the highest total score at the end of the game wins.

      FAQs:

      Q: What happens if there is a tie?
      A: In the event of a tie, the tied players each draw a new Challenge Card and perform the challenge. The player who completes the challenge first wins.

      Q: Can players help each other during challenges?
      A: No, players must complete challenges on their own without assistance from others.

      Q: Can a player skip a challenge?
      A: No, players cannot skip a challenge. They must attempt each challenge they draw.

      Q: What if a player draws a Challenge Card they can't perform due to physical limitations?
      A: If a player has a physical limitation that prevents them from performing a challenge, they may draw a new card. This rule should be agreed upon by all players before the game starts.

      Q: Are there different difficulty levels for challenges?
      A: Yes, Challenge Cards have different difficulty levels indicated by the number of points. Higher points usually mean more difficult challenges.

      Q: Can we add our own challenges to the game?
      A: Yes, players are encouraged to add their own challenges to make the game more fun and personalized. Just make sure everyone agrees on the new challenges before they are added to the deck.

      Q: How long is the timer set for each challenge?
      A: The standard time for each challenge is one minute, but players can agree to change the duration before starting the game.

      Q: Can we play in teams?
      A: Yes, players can form teams if they prefer. In team play, teammates can assist each other during challenges. The team with the highest total score wins.

      Q: What if a challenge is unclear or causes disputes?
      A: If a challenge is unclear or causes disputes, players can vote to either modify the challenge for clarity or draw a new card. Majority vote rules.
