      Instructions

      "Skill" is a game that tests your dexterity and precision. Here's how to play:

      1. Setup:
      - Place the game board on a flat surface.
      - Distribute the game pieces equally among all players.

      2. Objective:
      - The goal is to move all your pieces from the starting area to the target zone as quickly and accurately as possible.

      3. Gameplay:
      - Players take turns flicking or sliding one piece at a time towards the target zone.
      - Each player must use only one finger to move their piece during their turn.
      - If a piece lands outside the play area, it is returned to the starting area.

      4. Scoring:
      - Points are awarded based on where the pieces land in the target zone.
      - The closer a piece is to the center of the target, the higher the points.
      - Pieces that touch the boundary lines of the target zone receive no points.

      5. Winning:
      - The game continues until all pieces have been played.
      - The player with the highest total score at the end of the game wins.

      6. Rules:
      - No touching or moving the board while a piece is in motion.
      - Players must wait for their turn and cannot interfere with opponents' pieces.

      Enjoy honing your precision and dexterity as you compete to become the master of "Skill"!
