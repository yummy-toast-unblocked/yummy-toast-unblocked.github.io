      Instructions

      Objective: Navigate Pacman through the maze to eat all the dots while avoiding the ghosts.

      Controls:
      - Arrow Keys: Move Pacman up, down, left, or right.

      Gameplay:
      1. Start the game and use the arrow keys to control Pacman.
      2. Eat all the small and large dots scattered throughout the maze.
      3. Avoid the colorful ghosts that move around the maze. If a ghost catches Pacman, you lose a life.
      4. Power Pellets (larger dots) temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing Pacman to eat them for extra points.
      5. Collect fruits and other bonus items for additional points.

      Winning: Clear all the dots from the maze to advance to the next level.

      Losing: Lose all your lives by being caught by the ghosts.
