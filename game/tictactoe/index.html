      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Each player has a set of 26 cards numbered 1-13 in two suits, red and black.
      * The goal is to get rid of all your cards by playing them in numbered sequences (e.g., 1-2-3) or sets (e.g., three of a kind).
      * Players take turns drawing from the deck or the discard pile.
      * If you draw a card that extends a sequence or set on the table, you can play it immediately.
      * If you cannot play a card, you must draw from the deck.
      * If the deck runs out, shuffle the discard pile to create a new deck.
      * The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can you play a card on any sequence or set?
      - No, you can only extend a sequence or set that is already on the table.
      * What happens if you cannot draw a card from the deck?
      - You lose your turn and the next player draws two cards.
      * Can you discard a card to the discard pile if you cannot play it?
      - Yes, but only if you have drawn a card from the deck on that turn.
      * What happens if a player has only one card left?
      - They must call out "Uno!". If they fail to do so and are caught, they must draw two cards.
