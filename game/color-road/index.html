      Instructions

      Objective:
      In Skill, the goal is to accumulate the highest score by successfully completing a series of increasingly difficult challenges.

      Setup:
      1. Each player selects a game piece and places it on the starting point.
      2. Shuffle the deck of challenge cards and place them face down in the center.
      3. Choose a timekeeper to manage the timer (if applicable).

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns drawing a challenge card from the deck.
      2. The player reads the challenge aloud and attempts to complete it within the given time limit.
      3. Challenges can include physical tasks, mental puzzles, or skill-based activities.
      4. If the player successfully completes the challenge, they score points as indicated on the card and move their piece forward on the board.
      5. If the player fails, no points are awarded, and the turn passes to the next player.
      6. The game continues until all challenge cards have been drawn or a predetermined number of rounds have been played.

      Winning:
      The player with the highest score at the end of the game is declared the winner.

      FAQs:

      Q: How many players can play Skill?
      A: Skill can be played by 2 to 6 players.

      Q: What happens if a player draws a challenge they cannot perform due to a physical limitation?
      A: If a player cannot perform a challenge due to a physical limitation, they may draw a new challenge card without penalty.

      Q: Are there different difficulty levels for the challenges?
      A: Yes, the challenge cards are categorized by difficulty levels. Players can choose to play with mixed levels or select a specific difficulty based on their preference.

      Q: Can players skip a challenge if they think it's too difficult?
      A: No, players must attempt every challenge they draw. However, they can opt to pass their turn, receiving no points, and the next player proceeds.

      Q: Is there a time limit for each challenge?
      A: Yes, each challenge card specifies a time limit within which the task must be completed.

      Q: What if there's a tie at the end of the game?
      A: In the event of a tie, the tied players each draw one final challenge card. The player who completes their challenge the fastest wins.

      Q: Can the game be played with teams?
      A: Yes, Skill can be adapted for team play by having team members collaborate on completing challenges.

      Q: Is there a recommended age for playing Skill?
      A: Skill is recommended for players aged 8 and up, but some challenges may be more suitable for older players.
