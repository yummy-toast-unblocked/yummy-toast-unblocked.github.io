      Instructions

      Gameplay:
      1. Start by dragging and dropping puzzle pieces onto the board.
      2. Match pieces of the same shape and color to create rows or columns of three or more.
      3. Matched pieces disappear, creating space for new pieces to fall.
      4. Rotate pieces by tapping or clicking on them.
      5. Earn points by clearing multiple rows or columns at once.
      6. The game ends when there are no more moves available.
      FAQs:
      * How do I score points? By clearing rows or columns of three or more matching pieces.
      * Can I rotate the pieces? Yes, tap or click on a piece to rotate it.
      * What happens when I clear multiple rows or columns? You earn bonus points and create more space for new pieces to fall.
      * How can I prevent the game from ending? Keep clearing rows or columns to make space for new pieces.
      * What's the goal of the game? To score as many points as possible before the game ends.
