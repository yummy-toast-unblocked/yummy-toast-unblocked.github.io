      Instructions

      Instructions:
      1. Shuffle the deck and deal 5 cards to each player.
      2. Place the remaining deck face down in the center of the table to form the draw pile.
      3. Flip the top card of the draw pile to create the discard pile.
      4. Players take turns drawing a card from either the draw pile or the discard pile.
      5. If a player draws a card that matches the rank or suit of the top card on the discard pile, they can play it on top.
      6. If a player cannot play a card, they draw a card from the draw pile.
      7. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can I play a card on top of an Ace with any other card? No, you can only play an Ace on top of another Ace.
      * What happens if the draw pile runs out? Shuffle the discard pile (except for the top card) to create a new draw pile.
      * Can I play a card on top of a card that has already been played? No, you can only play cards on top of the top card on the discard pile.
