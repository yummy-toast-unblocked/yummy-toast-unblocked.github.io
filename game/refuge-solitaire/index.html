      Instructions

      Instructions:
      1. Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards to each player.
      2. Place the remaining deck face down in the center to form the draw pile.
      3. Turn the top card of the draw pile face up to start the discard pile.
      4. On your turn, you can do one of the following:
      - Play a card from your hand that matches the number or suit of the top card on the discard pile.
      - Draw a card from the draw pile.
      5. If you cannot play a card, you must draw one from the draw pile.
      6. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
      FAQs:
      * Can I play a card that matches the number but not the suit?
      - Yes.
      * What happens if I draw a card that I can play immediately?
      - You can play it on the same turn.
      * Can I draw multiple cards from the draw pile?
      - No, you can only draw one card per turn.
      * What happens if the draw pile runs out?
      - Shuffle the discard pile (except for the top card) to create a new draw pile.
      * Can I play a card that matches the suit but not the number?
      - No.
