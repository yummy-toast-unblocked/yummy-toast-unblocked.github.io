      Instructions

      Gameplay:
      * Gather 3-6 players.
      * Deal 5 cards to each player face down.
      * Place the remaining deck in the center as the draw pile.
      * Flip the top card of the draw pile to start the discard pile.
      * The player to the left of the dealer starts the game.
      * On your turn, you must either:
      * Match the number, suit, or color of the top card on the discard pile with a card from your hand and place it on top.
      * If you cannot match, draw a card from the draw pile.
      * If the drawn card matches, you can play it immediately.
      * If you have matched all 5 cards from your hand, you have gone out and won the round.
      * The first player to go out wins the round.
      FAQs:
      * Can I draw multiple cards? No, you can only draw one card per turn.
      * What happens if I cannot match or draw a card? Your turn ends and the next player takes their turn.
      * Can I play a wild card on top of another wild card? Yes, you can.
      * How many wild cards are in the deck? There are 4 wild cards and 4 wild draw four cards in the deck.
      * Can I challenge a player's play? Yes, if you believe a player played a card illegally, you can challenge them. If the challenge is successful, the player who played the card draws two cards. If the challenge is unsuccessful, you draw two cards.
