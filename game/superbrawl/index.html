      Objective:
      In "", players aim to collect as many points as possible by completing various challenges and tasks within the game.

      Setup:
      1. Shuffle the deck of Task Cards and place them in the center of the table.
      2. Give each player a Score Sheet and a pen or pencil.
      3. Determine the starting player by rolling a die.

      Gameplay:
      1. On your turn, draw a Task Card from the deck and read it aloud.
      2. Complete the task described on the card within the given time limit (if applicable).
      3. If you successfully complete the task, mark the points on your Score Sheet.
      4. Pass the deck to the next player.

      Winning:
      The game ends when all Task Cards have been drawn and completed. The player with the highest score on their Score Sheet wins.

      FAQs:

      Q: What happens if I fail to complete a task?
      A: If you fail to complete a task, you do not earn any points for that turn. The next player then takes their turn.

      Q: Can I attempt the same task more than once?
      A: No, each task can only be attempted once by each player.

      Q: What if there is a tie in points at the end of the game?
      A: In the case of a tie, the players involved in the tie draw one additional Task Card each. The player who successfully completes their task first wins.

      Q: Are there any penalties for failing a task?
      A: There are no penalties for failing a task, but you miss the opportunity to earn points for that turn.

      Q: Can I skip a turn if I don't want to attempt the task?
      A: No, you must attempt the task on your turn. Skipping a turn is not allowed.

      Q: How long is the time limit for timed tasks?
      A: The time limit for each timed task is specified on the Task Card. If no time limit is mentioned, you have 2 minutes to complete the task.

      Q: Can we modify the rules or tasks?
      A: Yes, you can modify the rules or tasks to better fit your group's preferences, as long as all players agree on the changes before starting the game.
