      Instructions

      In Where Is My Cat, your goal is to find the hidden cat in each level. Follow these steps to play:

      1. Start the Game: Launch the game and select a level.
      2. Examine the Scene: Look carefully at the picture displayed on the screen. The cat is hidden somewhere within it.
      3. Use Hints: If you get stuck, you can use hints to get clues about the cat's location. Hints are limited, so use them wisely.
      4. Tap the Cat: Once you spot the cat, tap on it to complete the level.
      5. Advance to the Next Level: After finding the cat, move on to the next, more challenging level.

      Tips:
      - Pay attention to details and color patterns that might reveal the cat's hiding spot.
      - Some levels may require you to move objects or solve mini-puzzles to uncover the cat.

      Enjoy the challenge and have fun finding your cat!
