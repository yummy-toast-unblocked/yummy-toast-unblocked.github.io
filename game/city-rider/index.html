      Instructions

      Objective: Score 41 points or more by adding up the values of cards in your hand.

      Setup:
      1. Use a standard deck of 52 cards.
      2. Remove the jokers.
      3. Each player is dealt 4 cards.

      Card Values:
      - Aces are worth 11 points.
      - Face cards (Kings, Queens, Jacks) are worth 10 points each.
      - Number cards are worth their face value (2-10).

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns in a clockwise direction.
      2. On your turn, draw one card from the deck or the discard pile.
      3. Discard one card to the discard pile, keeping your hand at 4 cards.
      4. If your hand totals 41 points or more, you can call "A Small World Cup" to end the game.
      5. The game also ends when the deck is depleted.

      Scoring:
      1. If a player calls "A Small World Cup" and their hand totals 41 points or more, they win the game.
      2. If the game ends with the deck depleted, the player with the highest total in their hand wins.

      Winning:
      - The first player to call "A Small World Cup" with a valid hand wins.
      - If no one calls it, the player with the highest total wins when the deck is depleted.
