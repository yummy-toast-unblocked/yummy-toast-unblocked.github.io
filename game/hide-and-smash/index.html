      - Yes, the game automatically saves your progress at the end of each level.

      3. What happens if I lose all my health?
      - If your health bar is depleted, the game ends. You can restart from the beginning of the current level.

      4. How can I earn more points?
      - Defeat enemies, complete objectives, and collect power-ups to earn more points.

      - Yes, you can unlock different characters by earning points and completing specific challenges.
