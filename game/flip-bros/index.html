      Instructions

      Objective:
      The goal of the game is to be the first player to collect all the hidden gems scattered across the board.

      Setup:
      1. Place the game board on a flat surface.
      2. Shuffle the gem cards and place them face down in a draw pile.
      3. Each player chooses a character token and places it on the starting space.
      4. Decide the order of play by rolling a die; the highest roller goes first.

      Gameplay:
      1. On your turn, roll the die and move your character token the corresponding number of spaces in any direction.
      2. If you land on a space with an action icon, follow the instructions for that action (e.g., draw a gem card, lose a turn, move forward/backward).
      3. If you land on a space occupied by another player, you may challenge them to a duel by rolling the die. The highest roller wins the duel and takes a gem card from the loser.
      4. Continue taking turns in a clockwise direction.

      Winning the Game:
      The first player to collect all five different colored gem cards wins the game.

      FAQs:

      1. What happens if I roll a number higher than the remaining spaces to the end of the board?
      - If you roll a number higher than the remaining spaces to the end of the board, you must move to the end and then backtrack the excess spaces.

      2. Can I move in any direction on my turn?
      - Yes, you can move in any direction (forwards, backwards, or sideways) as long as you do not move diagonally.

      3. What do I do if I draw a gem card I already have?
      - If you draw a duplicate gem card, place it at the bottom of the draw pile and draw again until you get a new gem card.

      4. Can I skip a space with an action icon?
      - No, if you land on a space with an action icon, you must perform the action specified.

      5. What if two players have the same number of different colored gem cards at the end of a round?
      - In the event of a tie, the players involved in the tie must roll the die. The highest roller wins the game.

      6. Is there a limit to how many times I can duel another player?
      - No, there is no limit. You can duel any player you land on as many times as the opportunity arises during the game.
