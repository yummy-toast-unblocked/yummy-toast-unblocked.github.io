      Instructions

      Objective:
      Race your car through various tracks, avoid obstacles, and reach the finish line as quickly as possible.

      Setup:
      1. Choose your vehicle from the available options.
      2. Select a track to race on.

      Controls:
      - Accelerate: Press the "Up" arrow key or "W".
      - Brake/Reverse: Press the "Down" arrow key or "S".
      - Steer Left: Press the "Left" arrow key or "A".
      - Steer Right: Press the "Right" arrow key or "D".
      - Nitro Boost: Press the "Spacebar" (if available).

      Gameplay:
      1. Start your car and accelerate to begin the race.
      2. Steer your car to navigate through the track, avoiding obstacles and other racers.
      3. Use the Nitro Boost strategically to gain speed on straight paths.
      4. Complete the required number of laps and cross the finish line.
      5. Your race time will be recorded; aim for the fastest time.

      Winning:
      - The player with the fastest completion time wins the race.
