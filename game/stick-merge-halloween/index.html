      Description

      5.0 (3318 Reviews)

      Rooftop Snipers is a thrilling action game that pits you against a friend or AI in intense one-on-one battles atop towering buildings. Engage in fast-paced arcade combat where precision and timing are your best allies. With its pixel-art graphics and simple controls, Rooftop Snipers offers an addictive and competitive experience for players of all skill levels. Dodge, shoot, and knock your opponent off the roof to claim victory in this exhilarating shooter showdown!


      Instructions

      Rooftop Snipers is a fast-paced 2-player game where you control a sniper on a rooftop, trying to knock your opponent off the platform. The first player to push their opponent off the roof 5 times wins.

      Controls:

      - Player 1:
      - W - Jump
      - E - Shoot

      - Player 2:
      - I - Jump
      - O - Shoot

      Gameplay:

      1. Start the Game: Choose to play against another player or the computer.
      2. Objective: Use jumps and shots to knock your opponent off the rooftop.
      3. Jump: Press the jump key to dodge enemy shots and reposition yourself.
      4. Shoot: Press the shoot key to fire your weapon. Aim to push your opponent off the edge.
      5. Rounds: Each time a player falls off the roof, the other player scores a point.
      6. Winning: The game ends when one player reaches 5 points first.

      Tips:

      - Timing your jumps and shots is crucial for avoiding enemy fire and successfully hitting your opponent.
      - Use the environment and movement to your advantage to outmaneuver your opponent.
