      Instructions

      Objective:
      Navigate your car through various levels, avoiding obstacles and collecting points to achieve the highest score possible.

      Instructions:
      1. Start the game by selecting 'Play' from the main menu.
      2. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard:
      - Up Arrow: Accelerate
      - Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
      - Left Arrow: Steer Left
      - Right Arrow: Steer Right
      3. Avoid obstacles such as other cars, barriers, and roadblocks.
      4. Collect coins and power-ups scattered throughout the levels to earn points and gain advantages.
      5. Complete each level by crossing the finish line within the time limit.
      6. Progress through increasingly difficult levels to improve your skills and score.

      FAQs:

      Q: How do I control the car?
      A: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard. Up Arrow to accelerate, Down Arrow to brake or reverse, Left Arrow to steer left, and Right Arrow to steer right.

      Q: What happens if I hit an obstacle?
      A: Hitting obstacles will slow you down and may cause you to lose points or power-ups. Repeated collisions can end the game.

      Q: How can I earn points?
      A: You can earn points by collecting coins, completing levels quickly, and performing special actions like drifting or jumping.

      Q: Are there any power-ups? What do they do?
      A: Yes, there are various power-ups like speed boosts, shields, and extra time. Speed boosts make you go faster, shields protect you from obstacles, and extra time extends your level timer.

      Q: Can I customize my car?
      A: Yes, you can customize your car by selecting the 'Garage' option from the main menu. You can change the color, upgrade parts, and add accessories using the points you've earned.

      Q: What should I do if I'm having trouble with a level?
      A: Try practicing to get better control over the car, or use power-ups strategically. You can also replay previous levels to earn more points and upgrade your car.

      Q: How do I pause the game?
      A: Press the 'P' key or click on the pause button on the screen to pause the game.

      Q: Is there a multiplayer mode?
      A: Yes, you can play with friends by selecting the 'Multiplayer' option from the main menu. You can race against each other and see who finishes first.

      Q: How do I resume the game after pausing?
      A: Press the 'P' key again or click the 'Resume' button on the pause menu to continue playing.

      Q: What if I run out of time before finishing a level?
      A: If you run out of time, the game will end, and you'll have to restart the level. Try to be quicker and avoid unnecessary delays to complete the level within the time limit.
