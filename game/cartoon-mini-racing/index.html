      Race your car to the finish line while avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups to achieve the highest score.

      Setup:
      1. Launch the game on your device.
      2. Select your car and customize it if options are available.
      3. Choose your race track.

      Controls:
      1. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer the car:
      - Up Arrow/W: Accelerate
      - Down Arrow/S: Brake/Reverse
      - Left Arrow/A: Steer left
      - Right Arrow/D: Steer right
      2. Spacebar: Use nitro boost (if available).

      1. Start the race when the countdown ends.
      2. Navigate through the track, avoiding obstacles like other cars, barriers, and road hazards.
      3. Collect power-ups scattered around the track for boosts, shields, or extra points.
      4. Complete the required number of laps to finish the race.
      5. Cross the finish line in the best position possible.

      Winning:
      1. The player with the fastest time or the highest score wins the race.
      2. Points are awarded based on performance, including finishing position, time, and power-ups collected.
