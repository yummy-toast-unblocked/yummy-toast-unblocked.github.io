      Instructions

      Instructions:
      * Objective: Defend your base from waves of stickman enemies.
      * Controls:
      * Use the mouse to select towers and place them strategically.
      * Left-click towers to upgrade them.
      * Right-click towers to sell them for a refund.
      * Towers:
      * Build a variety of towers, each with unique abilities and costs.
      * Towers automatically attack enemies within their range.
      * Enemies:
      * Waves of stickman enemies will approach from multiple paths.
      * Enemies come in different types, each with varying health and speed.
      * Upgrades:
      * Upgrade towers to increase their damage, range, or abilities.
      * Upgrades cost money, which is earned by killing enemies.
      * Levels:
      * Progress through multiple levels, each with its own unique challenges.
      * Each level introduces new enemy types and obstacles.
      FAQs:
      * Q: How do I place towers?
      * A: Click on a tower icon in the bottom right corner, then click on the desired location on the map.
      * Q: How do I upgrade towers?
      * A: Left-click on a tower and then click on the "Upgrade" button.
      * Q: How do I sell towers?
      * A: Right-click on a tower and then click on the "Sell" button.
      * Q: What happens if I lose?
      * A: You will need to restart the level.
      * Q: How many levels are there?
      * A: There are 10 levels in total.
