      Instructions

      Objective: Be the first player to reach the finish line by navigating the track and avoiding obstacles.

      Setup:
      1. Each player chooses a car token.
      2. Place the car tokens at the starting line on the game board.
      3. Shuffle and distribute the deck of action cards evenly among players.

      Gameplay:
      1. Players take turns in clockwise order.
      2. On your turn, draw an action card from your deck and follow the instructions.
      - Action cards may include moves (e.g., "Move forward 3 spaces"), obstacles (e.g., "Lose a turn"), or special abilities (e.g., "Swap places with another player").
      3. After completing the action on the card, discard it to a common discard pile.
      4. Players continue to take turns, drawing and playing cards, and moving their car tokens along the track.

      Winning the Game:
      1. The first player to reach the finish line exactly wins the game.
      2. If a player draws a card that would move them past the finish line, they must wait until their next turn to draw a new card and attempt to reach the finish line exactly.

      Additional Rules:
      - If you land on a space occupied by another player, move your car token to the next available open space.
      - Certain spaces on the board may have special effects (e.g., turbo boosts, pit stops) that impact your movement or abilities. Follow the instructions on the board when landing on these spaces.

      Have fun and drive safely!
